Get Involved: Speak at Council

Community engagement is vital to our democratic process. You can participate in city council meetings in several ways:

  • In-Person: Register at the kiosk in the city hall lobby before the meeting starts.
  • Remote: Sign Up online to speak via telephone or videoconference.
  • Written: Submits comments via email or the online portal for council review.
